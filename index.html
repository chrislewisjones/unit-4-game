Dubious Pizza

You will be given a random Topping Target at the start of each game.

There are four toppings below, by clicking on a topping you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your score goes above the random number.

The value of each topping is unknown, you will have to click on it the find out.

Each time the game starts, the target and toppings will change in value.
